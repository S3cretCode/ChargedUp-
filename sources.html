Linden & Reddy (2010)

Linden, D., & Reddy, T. B. (Eds.). (2010). Linden's handbook of batteries (4th ed.). McGraw-Hill.

Used for: Alkaline cell chemistry, discharge characteristics, internal resistance values. This is the definitive reference for battery technology.

In-text citation: (Linden & Reddy, 2010)

Energizer Technical Bulletin (2018)

Energizer Holdings, Inc. (2018). Energizer E91 AA battery product datasheet. Retrieved December 20th from https://data.energizer.com/pdfs/e91.pdf

Used for: Commercial AA battery specifications, capacity ratings (mAh), internal resistance data, and discharge curves for different load conditions.

In-text citation: (Energizer, 2018)

Atkins Physical Chemistry (2017)

Atkins, P., de Paula, J., & Keeler, J. (2017). Atkins' physical chemistry (11th ed.). Oxford University Press.

Used for: Electrochemistry fundamentals, Faraday's laws of electrolysis, standard electrode potentials, and thermodynamic principles of galvanic cells.

In-text citation: (Atkins et al., 2017)

NIST CODATA (2018)

Tiesinga, E., Mohr, P. J., Newell, D. B., & Taylor, B. N. (2021). CODATA recommended values of the fundamental physical constants: 2018. Reviews of Modern Physics, 93(2), 025010.

Used for: Faraday constant (F = 96,485.33212 C/mol), fundamental constants.

In-text citation: (NIST, 2018)
