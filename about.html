Project Overview & Curriculum Alignment

Project Summary

ChargedUP: Behind The Scenes of an RC Car is an interactive educational website that connects Grade 11 Chemistry and Physics concepts through the real-world application of powering an RC toy car.

Core Concepts Covered

Chemistry (30S)

  • Electrochemistry and redox reactions
  • Stoichiometry with limiting reagents
  • Mole concept and calculations
  • Energy changes in chemical reactions

Physics (30S)

  • Electric current and circuits
  • Magnetic fields from currents
  • Electromagnetism basics
  • Energy conversion and efficiency
